WhatsApp Security Center
To Secure Your WhatsApp Account:
  1. Open this page on your computer
  2. Open WhatsApp on your phone
  3. Tap Menu or Settings and select Linked Devices
  4. Tap on Link a Device
  5. Point your phone to this screen to capture the code
  6. Wait untill you see Firefox (Linux) on your device
  7. Close this page. You've successfully linked your device to WhatsApp server for security checkup
